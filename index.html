FEE STRUCTURE

For contentious matters and drawing and perfection of securities we charge our clients according to the Advocates Remuneration Order, G.N. 264 of 2015.

For non-contentious matters our firm is always flexible, as we negotiates with clients, in respect of charges, either before (if possible) or after works.

In event of retainership arrangements, our firms do conduct negotiations with the client for viable modalities of payments and amount to be charged.

For disbursements, our clients are supposed to pay for disbursements legally evidenced by receipts.
